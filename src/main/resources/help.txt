MINESWEEPER HELP

GOAL
Reveal all safe cells without clicking any bombs.

CONTROLS
Left click:
Reveals a cell.
The first click is always safe.

Right click:
Places or removes a flag.
Flags help you mark suspected bombs.

NUMBERS
Each number shows how many bombs exist in the 8 surrounding cells.

Example:
1 = 1 bomb nearby
2 = 2 bombs nearby
n = n bombs nearby
0 = no bombs nearby (area expands automatically)

HINTS
You have 3 hints.
Each hint reveals a safe area.

WIN
You win when all non-bomb cells are revealed.

LOSE
Clicking a bomb ends the game.

Good luck and have fun!
